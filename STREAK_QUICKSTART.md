## Tips for Success

### Week 1: Build the Habit
- Set phone alarms for each goal
- Morning alarm: Weigh yourself
- Afternoon alarm: Exercise time
- Evening alarm: Check progress

### Week 2: Optimize
- Front-load water (2L by lunch)
- Pre-plan meals for calorie accuracy
- Schedule exercise at same time daily
- Weigh in first thing every morning

### Week 3+: Autopilot
- Habits now feel natural
- Less thinking required
- Streak becomes motivating
- Don't break the chain!
